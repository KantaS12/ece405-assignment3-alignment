\section*{Problem (compute\_grpo\_clip\_loss): GRPO-Clip loss (2 points)} % [cite: 699]
Deliverable: Implement a method \texttt{compute\_grpo\_clip\_loss} that computes the per-token GRPO-Clip loss. % [cite: 700]

\textbf{Answer:}

I compute the per-token ratio $r_t = \exp(\log\pi_\theta - \log\pi_\text{old})$, clip it to $[1-\varepsilon, 1+\varepsilon]$, then take the element-wise max of $-A \cdot r_t$ and $-A \cdot \text{clip}(r_t)$. This keeps the policy from making large updates and straying too far from the rollout policy. I also log the fraction of tokens clipped.
